Discover American Fork

Explosions

Sometimes houses explode in American Fork, and it's something we've come to embrace. Rent an AirBnB and you could be the lucky next victim!

American Fork Canyon

American Fork Canyon offers windy roads, hikes and trails, climbing, caves, and more. Come take a dip in Tibble Fork reservoir!

History

American Fork has a rich history dating back to 1847. Come see our historical buildings, parks, and learn more about our history!

Hospital

American Fork is home to state of the art health facilities. No matter your ailment, we have the facilities to fix you up (maybe).

Downtown

Explore Downtown American Fork and see the vast variety of cafe's and shops. We have what you're looking for!
